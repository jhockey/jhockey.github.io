About me and my work

Projects

While no two projects are alike, I approach each with a consistent design thinking philosophy and adapt the processes and tools I use to address the diverse challenges presented by each problem I encounter. Below are some of the projects I have had the opportunity to work on.

Project HIU

2023

I traveled to Indonesia with a team of designers to aid Project Hiu in understanding how to help local fishermen shift from shark hunting to sustainable tourism through the implementation of design thinking into the organisations opperations.

View Project

ABOA (Abled by one another)

2023

I designed an inclusive platform for people in Australia impacted by disabilities to facilitate greater community engagement within the disabled population. The platform aims to foster meaningful exchanges by offering a place for disability-driven discussion and provides community tested knowledge and experiences for various locations and businesses, ultimately enabling people to get outside and enjoy life more.

View Project

Chiropractic Care Australia

Ongoing

Through the application of design strategy, I developed and executed physical and digital design and service systems aimed at enhancing and simplifying the patient's user journey, ultimately resulting in a greater number of positive experiences with the company's services.
